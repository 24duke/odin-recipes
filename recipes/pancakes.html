Pancakes

What is a Pancake

Pancakes are a delightful breakfast staple cherished for their fluffy texture and versatile flavor. Made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder, pancakes are cooked on a griddle or skillet until golden brown. They offer a perfect canvas for a variety of toppings and fillings, from classic maple syrup and butter to fresh berries, whipped cream, or even chocolate chips. Whether enjoyed as a stack with a drizzle of honey or layered with fruit and yogurt, pancakes provide a warm and satisfying start to the day, combining comfort and culinary creativity in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare dry ingredients
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients
  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
  4. Preheat the Cooking Surface
  5. Cook the Pancakes
  6. Serve
